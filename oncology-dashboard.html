Emerging Therapeutic Modalities
🎯
Antibody-Drug Conjugates
$13.5B
CAGR ~9–10% → $33B by 2035
15 FDA-approved ADCs. Fastest-growing modality — 284 new oncology trials in 2024. Enhertu commands 23% of ADC market. "Biological missiles" replacing chemo.
☢️
Radioligand Therapy
~$2.5B
Trial starts tripled 2020–2024
Pluvicto (lutetium-177 PSMA) leading; Lutathera for NETs. Novartis dominant. 4 major RLT deals >$1B each in H1 2024. Expanding beyond prostate cancer.
🧬
CAR-T Cell Therapy
~$3B
Projected $14B by 2029
7 FDA-approved CAR-T therapies (Jan 2025). Leaders: Kymriah, Yescarta, Breyanzi, Carvykti. J&J Phase II/III next-gen CAR-T for DLBCL started Jan 2025.
🔗
Bispecific Antibodies
$9B
3 new FDA approvals in 2024
~250 new trials started in 2024, up 256% since 2019. Tecvayli, Talvey, Elrexfio in myeloma. CD3×CD20 and BCMA targets leading. Bridging T cells to tumors.
🛡️
Checkpoint Inhibitors
$55B+
Dominant; biosimilar risk 2028+
PD-1/PD-L1 maintains absolute dominance. Keytruda approved for 49 US indications. Biosimilar pressure expected 2028 at Keytruda's patent cliff.
✂️
CRISPR / Gene Therapy
Emerging
Viral vector mfg CAGR 24.6%
Base editing trials ongoing (Beam Therapeutics). CRISPR-Cas9 enabling targeted cancer cell modification. Clinical translation accelerating in hematologic malignancies.
💊
PARP Inhibitors
~$4B
BRCA-mutated tumors focus
Lynparza (olaparib, AZ/MSD), Zejula, Talzenna in breast/ovarian. Now expanding into prostate cancer. Combination with immunotherapy being explored.
🤖
AI-Driven Drug Design
Accelerating
Reshaping discovery timelines
AI used in biomarker identification, target discovery and clinical trial optimization. Chinese biopharma executed $30B in oncology licensing in 2024, accelerating AI-driven pipelines.
